Hello, I'm Mohd Adil Hossain

Web Developer & Graphic Designer

I'm in my final year of Creative Computing at Bath Spa University, where I love merging web development and design to build unique digital experiences. I've picked up strong skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript along the way. I’m also into photography, video editing with Capcut, and making eye-catching graphics with Canva and Figma.

I like experimenting with creative coding and interactive web projects, always searching for new ways to make websites useful and fun. My goal is to blend technical know-how with a good eye for design, so I can create sites that are both easy to use and visually appealing.

I work with Python, Java, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, MySQL, and MongoDB. I enjoy building responsive websites and apps that are practical but also enjoyable. I’ve used Unity, GitHub, Figma, and Android Studio to bring different ideas to life and make projects that are interactive and user-friendly. Right now, I’m excited to keep learning, work with others, and look for internships and projects in web development, Python, and UI/UX design.
